Welcome


Greetings! I am a PhD candidate in the Government Department at the University of Texas at Austin. I specialize in Comparative Politics and Political Methodology. My substantive research agenda focuses on contentious politics and politics in East Asia. My methodological research develops and employs game-theoretical and experimental methods to the study of contentious politics. During my studies at UT Austin, I have been working as a teaching assistant for various Statistics and Data Science courses.

Apart from research and teaching, I enjoy playing and listening to music, especially classical music. I also like travelling and discovering the beauty of mother nature.
